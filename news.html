Cryptography

  • Schneier on Security: News Bruce Schneier is a public-interest technologist, working at the intersection of security, technology, and people. He's been writing about security issues on his blog since 2004 and on his newsletter since 1998.

  • Schneier on Security: Crypto-Gram Newsletter. A free monthly newsletter digest from his posts

  • ePrint Archive: It is a major hub for cryptographics paper in the communauty. You can search for paper by tags and release date.

  • r/crypto: Reddit main sub for cryptography. You can ask questions, find recent plublished paper and chat with the community.

  • Cryptohack's discord server A wonderful community of cryptographers, you can ask for help, follow the news, share memes and a lot more!
